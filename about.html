Creating a comsumption visualization

How to read the mapping:

  • Each third of the circle maps one of the three variables (Hot Water, Cold Water, Energy)
  • The circle maps up to the four last days/hours to enable comparison
  • The inner-most circle is the most recent day, and the outer ones are the farther past days
  • The saturation of the color tells how much of that good was used in the period of time
  • If one of the quadrants is white, it means no activity was recorded for that given period

Technologies used:

  • D3.js
  • jQuery
  • HTML Canvas
